About Us

U.S. Artificial Intelligence was founded by Gordon Kamer in 2023. Before founding the company, Gordon graduated with a degree in Computer Science from Harvard College. While there, he researched machine learning methods for zero-shot image classification, declarative approaches to building neural network architectures, and other topics in AI.

He previously worked for the Washington-based consultancy Long-Term Strategy Group, where he advised the Department of Defense on how AI may impact national security. Following his time at LTSG, Gordon worked in the financial services industry at a quantitative trading firm.
