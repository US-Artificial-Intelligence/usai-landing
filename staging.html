Bring AI to Your Business
Artificial Intelligence presents an incredible opportunity for businesses to outpace competitors. It can let one person do the work of ten or more. However, many companies have difficulty discerning the cases when AI is transformative from the cases when AI is overhyped.
We help instutitions identify areas where AI provides an edge, in addition to the areas where it won't. Once we've idenitifed areas where an AI-powered solution adds value, we will build out a custom tool for your company.
1.1
Automate Your Proposal Process
Using an underlying AI model similar to ChatGPT, our AI-powered RFP Responder can automatically retrieve relevant information from your company documents and use that information to generate answers to questions on new RFPs. While the new question might be unanswered in the previous docs, the model can pull together different resources and usually give a reasonable response. You can work with the AI side-by-side, providing it with additional information or steering its responses as you go.

Who We Are
U.S. Artificial Intelligence was founded by Gordon Kamer in 2023. Before founding the company, Gordon graduated with a degree in Computer Science from Harvard College.
